I’ll be the Losing Heroine in Every World

Sep 19, 2024

Nanami — Sakurasou no Pet na Kanojo (2012)

“But I can only be the losing heroine so many times before it kills me you know?”

What’s my most prevalent red flag? I’m obsessive, possessive, and I get jealous extremely easily. Sorry, but I can’t help it.

It’s undiagnosed anxious attachment style and whatever else I have. It’s always been like this for me. I find my favorite person and I latch on to them like my life depends on it. Usually it feels like my life does depend on them. I start thinking about them every waking hour of my life. I wonder what they’re doing, if they’re ok, and most importantly, who they’re talking to. That jealousy and yearning I feel when they’re talking to another person will always kill me on the inside. I know they must like that person more than me, so why am I even here? You should spend every minute of your life with me, not that person.

I’ve been feeling like this recently despite everything. I shouldn’t feel like this, but I do. How do I stop this feeling? It’s not rhetorical, if anyone knows, please let me know.

I remember a year and a half ago, I felt this same way too with a person that I really had no business getting so enamored with. I would walk around my high school only thinking about them because I didn’t really talk to anyone. There was nothing to distract me from you.

Minorin — Toradora (2008)

That’s another thing about me too. I fall in love too easily. I wouldn’t call it love though. It’s much more like I’m obsessed with the idea of falling in love. If you give me just a little bit of attention when I need it the most, I’ll fall head over heels for you and become obsessed with this idealistic version of you that isn’t real. And then you’ll leave when you find it how I am. That’s not something you want is it?

Yet, every single person who has given me this attention remains in my mind everyday. Their messages and the feeling they gave me so clear in my mind as if it was just yesterday when they left. You were all so kind to give me a little bit of your time despite me being who I am. You all gave me a little happiness for however long you stayed. 3 months? 1 month? A week? Each one meant so much to me. Even if you all eventually left, I don’t blame you. I’d leave me too.

I know why I’m like this too. It’s a result of my insecurities that haunt my all the time. The constant feeling that I’m not good enough. It’ll always be here. The inconsistent parenting my parents gave me too. But I can’t really blame them can I? They were just working all the time to give me a better life. Nowadays, we have all this money and a much bigger house than we did when I was a child, but if I’m being honest, I’d trade all of this away to have been given some attention as a child that wasn’t in the form of abuse.

Is that selfish and abhorrent of me? Maybe. But living like this is torture. I don’t know how much longer I can do it. I would give away everything to rid myself of these mental illnesses. These constant thoughts that haunt me everyday. Please. I wish someone could make it stop. I’d do anything.

Yes, everything’s wrong with me and I’ll always be unlovable. I get it. So why can’t I learn? I will never get close with anyone again. Everyone will be kept at an arm’s distance and then I’ll complain and cry about how I have no one.

But it’s for the better isn’t it? I make everyone’s life worse. This world would be so much happier without me. I don’t want to hurt anyone ever again. It was never my intention. I just wanted to make people happy. So why can I only do the opposite?

In every world, every universe, every timeline, I’ll be the losing heroine. It’s my destiny and I’ll follow through with it.

Anna — Makeine: Too Many Losing Heroines (2024)
